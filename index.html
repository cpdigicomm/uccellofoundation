"Communities Helping Communities"

"Community Helping Community" It’ll blow your mind.

If you are looking for resources in the community, then the Uccello Foundation site is for you. Here we list websites of interest, including community agencies, events, and how to stay connected within the community. There are more resources available than most people realize. We will be continually adding to this resource listed on this site, so check back regularly. We encourage everyone to visit each page to see whats out there!

Every individual can play a role in building a supportive community, from introducing yourself to a neighbor to championing a block party. Activities can be small or large, focused on individual relationships or big picture initiatives. Is small talk not your thing? Acts of service are ways to connect, too! Consider ways to help the people around you, like taking a meal to someone who is sick or home-bound, or surprising a neighbor with a shoveled walk or weed-free flower bed.
